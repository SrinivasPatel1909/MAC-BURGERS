ABOUT US

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit MaC Burger restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 2008, MaC Burgers is the second largest fast food chain in the Mumbai. Our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 15 successful years.
